  \begin{equation}
      F\brak{s} = \frac{G\brak{s}}{1+G\brak{s}H\brak{s}}
  \end{equation}
Since it is a unity feed back system, $H\brak{s} = 1$, and now using the characteristic equation at $s_{1} = -1 + \j2$
\begin{equation}
1 + G\brak{s_{1}}H\brak{s_{1}} = 0
\end{equation}
\begin{equation}
 G\brak{s_{1}} = -1
\end{equation}
\begin{equation}
 \abs{G\brak{s_{1}}} = 1
\end{equation}
